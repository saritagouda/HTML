       SINCE EARLY DAYS OF HOTEL
                   AMRUT KARWAR

              Started in 1976 on a fateful day in October. It was a task taken
              up by our Grand father, Late. Srinivas Venkatraman Nilavar,
              who is the founding father of Amrut. He started this place to
              earning a daily buck for his Nine children & to meet their needs.
              A direct result of the Gold Control Act brought in by the Govt.
              at the time which terrified and forced families like ours to seek
              other means of livelihood. All the years of hard work and positive
              energy is what you see before you now as, Hotel Amrut.
